Ocean Cleanup

Pacific Ocean (10km Radius)

Join us in a global effort to rid the Pacific Ocean of harmful plastic waste. Volunteers will help collect debris, sort recyclables, and learn about the impact of pollution on marine life.

Forest Cleanup

Amazon Rainforest (100km Radius)

Help preserve one of the world's most vital ecosystems by participating in a forest cleanup in the Amazon Rainforest. Volunteers will remove litter, assist with habitat restoration, and engage in activities to support local conservation efforts.
